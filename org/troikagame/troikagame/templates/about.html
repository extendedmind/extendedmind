What is Troika Game?

Do you know what the person next to you wants to teach? If you knew, would you be able to arrange a session where the person could pass their knowledge? And if that happened, would you meet the person alone or could the knowledge be useful to more people? The biggest question to answer is the question of taking the first step. These are the questions that are preventing us from learning small things from each other: how to mend a bicycle, how to use a spreadsheet program, how to juggle etc. Small things that could make life easier and take very little time.

Troika is a method and a tool to answer these questions and overcome the simple - but demanding - obstacles in peer-to-peer learning and knowledge creation. Troika offers a platform where people can arrange a learning session and a way to communicate this session forward to other people interested in the matter. The purpose of Troika is make these things possible with the minimum level of organization. A troika will happen and will be considered a great success when three people gather.

Integral part of Troika is the recognition of effort. This happens through small and non-intrusive elements of game: badges and rewards. Hence the name Troika the Game!
