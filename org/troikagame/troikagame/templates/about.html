What is Troika Game?

Do you know what the person next to you wants to teach? What would be the funniest way to share this knowledge to you and other people ? Troika the Game is a tool developed for you to make learning together possible with just the right people around you. Troika helps you to easily organize a session for learning and passing the knowledge on.

Why do we need Troika for learning together? The biggest obstacle in sharing our knowledge is often to take the first step. Troubles in taking the first steps prevent us from learning small things from each other: how to mend a bicycle, how to use a spreadsheet program - how to juggle? Small things that could make life easier and take very little time.

Troika is a method and a tool to answer these questions and overcome the simple - but demanding - obstacles in peer-to-peer learning and knowledge creation. Troika offers

  1. a platform where people can arrange a learning session and
  2. a way to communicate this session forward to other people interested in the topic.
The purpose of Troika is to make these things possible with the minimum level of organization. A “troika” means a clique of three. When three people gather together at Troika event, the organized Troika will be considered as a great success!

What makes Troika truly a lot of fun is the recognition of effort. This happens through small and non-intrusive elements of game: badges and rewards. Hence the name Troika Game!

Troika builds on three simple premises:

  • You will fill a gap between people who know something and people who want to learn something.
  • You will engage a minimum of two (2) people.
  • You will talk about troika (Spread the word, advertise the Troika event and share the spirit).

Troika is not limited to any certain subject areas. We all know something worth teaching - think out of the box!

Ready to start learning? Join a Troika!

  1. Register as a user.
  2. Browse the list of active troika-events on the front page and find the ones you’re interested in. If there is no troika available on the topic you wish to learn, you may want set up your own “pending troika” and leave to "I will teach this" unclicked to request for a teacher.
  3. Sign up for an interesting troika by clicking the appropriate join link on the troika description page.
  4. Attend the troika and enjoy the experience of learning!
  5. Gain your badges and review.
  6. Recommend Troika to your friends and acquaintances.

Ready to start teaching? Set up your own Troika!

  1. Register as a user.
  2. Find a topic you wish to teach or a topic you want somebody to teach you to.
  3. Create a new pending troika!
  4. Wait for the first two to join your troika.
  5. Agree on the time and place and click "Activate troika" to make the troika active and publicly available.
  6. Attend the troika and enjoy the experience of learning!
  7. Gain your badges and reviews.
  8. Recommend Troika to your friends and acquaintances.
