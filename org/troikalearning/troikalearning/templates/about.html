{{ gettext('What is Troika?') }}

{{ gettext('Do you know what you could learn from the person next to you? What would be the funniest way to share this knowledge to you and other people? Troika is a tool developed for you to make learning together possible with just the right people around you. Troika helps you to easily organize a session for learning and passing the knowledge on.') }}

{{ gettext('Why do we need Troika for learning together? The biggest obstacle in sharing our knowledge or skills is often to take the first step. Troubles in taking the first steps prevent us from learning small things from each other: how to mend a bicycle, how to use a spreadsheet program - how to juggle? Small things that could make life easier and take very little time.') }}

{{ gettext('Troika is a method and a tool to answer these questions and overcome the simple - but demanding - obstacles in peer-to-peer learning and knowledge creation. Troika offers') }}

  1. {{ gettext('a platform where people can arrange a learning session and') }}
  2. {{ gettext('a way to communicate this session forward to other people interested in the topic.') }}
{{ gettext('The purpose of Troika is to make these things possible with the minimum level of organization. A \'troika\' means a clique of three. When three people gather together at Troika event, the organized Troika will be considered as a great success!') }}

{{ gettext('Troika builds on three simple premises:') }}

  • {{ gettext('You will fill a gap between people who know something and people who want to learn something.')}}
  • {{ gettext('You will engage a minimum of two (2) people.')}}
  • {{ gettext('You will talk about troika (Spread the word, advertise the Troika event and share the spirit).')}}

{{ gettext('Troika is not limited to any certain subject areas. We all know something worth sharing - think outside the box!')}}
