extended mind categories

impression

An impression is a text with which a person shares her thoughts on a specific matter. A note in the impression category has the lightest peer review standards.

instruction

An instruction is a helpful text which specifies the steps to accomplish. Examples of instructions are "howto" posts and recipies.

opinion piece

A note in the opinion piece category contains some partial view on a given subject matter. The text must contain good reasoning.

review

A review is an evaluation of a work. A review must follow good reasoning and contain insightful remarks and knowledge on the subject matter.

essay

An essay is a lengthy systematic discourse on a specific subject matter.

term paper

The term paper category is for argumentative studies that make thorough academic references.

academic paper

The most rigorous category of is the academic paper. An academic paper follows the norms of the scientific review process. Only selected cags with the most scientifically accomplished supervisors have support for academic papers.
